A little bit about myself

I was born in a tiny village called Yashkul located in the outskirts of Russia. I'm kalmyk and if you didn't know that there was such an ethnicity, don't feel bad. Most don't. We are a small group of nomad people who migrated from Mongolia to Russia in the early 1600s. Nowadays, it's the only region that practices Buddhism in Europe. There are quite a lot of interesting facts and if you're interested in knowing more, feel free to message me!

For awhile I've been struggling trying to find my "passion" and finally accidentally found it in programming. Once I knew what I wanted to do I jumped right in with no hesitation and decided to get a Masters degree in it at all costs. I was fortunate enough to continue working at my old job and study for awhile and loved every minute that I spent in school. Nothing compares to the satisfaction and a sense of accomplishment you get when your code finally does what you intended it to do. It's been a few days since I have graduated and I already miss it but super excited for what the next chapter will bring my way!

I dip my toes in anything I can. I love to do DIY projects and starting new hobbies. I like to be handy around the house so sometimes do odd jobs like painting, laying tiles, fixing toilets and such. I find small pleasures in finishing something that no one expects me to do.

I also love artsy stuff. I have a small instagram account with just a few examples of the work I do. I did all kinds of mediums but colored pencils are the new obssession of mine and will probably try digital painting when I get more free time.
